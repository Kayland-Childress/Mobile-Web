Barbering

Name Description Price
Traditional Barber Haircut Traditional Barbers Haircut includes cut and style. $30
Traditional Child Haircut A customized children's haircut designed by your stylist to fit your individual style and preferences. $30
Haircut and Beard Traditional Barbers Haircut includes cut and style. Beard service begins with a consultation and the initial shaping. Preshave oil is applied following a hot towel to open the pores. A straight razor is used to line up the top of the beard and remove unwanted neck growth. Finished with a cold towel to close the pores and oil/balm for scent and shaping. $60
Straight Razor Line Up This service includes a hot towel followed by a straight razor line up. A cold towel will be used to close the pores and a aftershave to complete the service. This can be used for either hair or beard but not both. If you would like both you will need to book the service twice. $30
Head Shave and Beard Trim Beard will be shaped to desired style then both head and beard will begin with a pre-shave oil and hot towel to open the pores. A single pass with the grain followed by a secondary pass against the grain to tighten the shave. Lastly a cold towel to close the pores with oils/balm to condition and shape. Aftershave is also applied for a as a prevention for unwanted ingrowns. $60
Beard Trim Service begins with a consultation and the initial shaping. Preshave oil is applied following a hot towel to open the pores. A straight razor is used to line up the top of the beard and remove unwanted neck growth. Finished with a cold towel to close the pores and oil/balm for scent and shaping. $30
Traditional Clean Shave The traditional Straight Razor Shave includes application of a steam towel to open up the pores and soften the hair follicle. Hot lather is applied and you will receive a first pass shave with the grain of the beard. A second application of hot lather follows, with second shave against the grain for a closer shave. A cool towel is applied to close the pores. $50
Hot Lather Head Shave This service will begin with the shorting of hair to remove growth. Followed by a pre-shave oils and a hot towel to open the pores. Hot lather will be applied followed by a one time pass with the grain before hot lather is applied for a second pass against the grain for a closer shave. Then oil is applied before closing the pores with a cold towel. $40
Barber Steam Facial Includes application of a steam towel to open up the pores, a facial wash, exfoliating scrub and a light facial massage. $60
Shampoo A shampoo service will consist of a scalp and hair wash with a blow-dry style. $15
